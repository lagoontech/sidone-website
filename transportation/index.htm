Transportation Services

NAS has a massive fleet of transportation catering to all its roadwork & logistical requirements from milling machine, trailers, trucks, pavers, bulldozers, wheel loaders, asphalt finisher, asphalt & earthwork rollers, motor graders, generators, air compressors, water tankers, asphalt anker, tack tanker, excavators, backhoe loaders, buses including a modern asphalt plant.

It has also a modern, centralized workshop facility responsible of maintaining these vehicles, providing timely electrical, mechanical, instrumentational repairs and maintenance services.
