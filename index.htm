How Does the Sidone Workflow Begin?

At Sidone Infrastructure, we follow a structured and efficient project workflow to ensure seamless execution and successful delivery. Here’s how the process begins:

Client Discussion

Initial meetings with the client to understand project requirements, budget, and expectations.

Architectural Plans: Section and Elevation Drawings

Detailed drawings showcasing vertical sections and external elevations of the structure for design clarity.

Structural Drawings

Blueprints outlining load-bearing elements, reinforcement details, and construction methodology.

MEP, HVAC, Networking, Fire Fighting, cctv, Landscape, Interior Drawings

Technical schematics for mechanical, electrical, plumbing, heating, ventilation, air conditioning, security, and aesthetic elements of the project.

Estimation, Rate Analysis and Budjet

Comprehensive cost breakdown, material/labor rate analysis, and budget planning for project execution.

Client Agreement

Legally binding document defining project scope, timelines, responsibilities, and financial terms between the client and Sidone Infrastructure.

Project Management ERP

A digital platform to streamline planning, scheduling, and resource allocation for efficient project execution.

Cash Flow Statement with Statutory Compliance

A financial report ensuring steady project funding while adhering to legal and regulatory requirements.
